Alternative Method - Get Entry IDs from Network Tab:

  1. Open your Google Form in a new tab
  2. Open Developer Tools (F12) and go to Network tab
  3. Check "Preserve log"
  4. Fill out and submit the form
  5. Look for "formResponse" in the network requests
  6. Click on it and check the "Form Data" section
  7. Copy the entry IDs from there
